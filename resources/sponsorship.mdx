## GitHub Sponsors vs OpenCollective

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between GitHub Sponsors and OpenCollective?">
    **GitHub Sponsors:**
    - Funds directly support [John Leider](https://github.com/sponsors/johnleider)
    - Support the ongoing development and maintenance of Vuetify
    - Direct contribution to the project creator
    
    **Open Collective:**
    - Funds are managed with transparent expenses
    - Used for compensating work and expenses for Core team members
    - Supports the broader Core team
    
    **Recognition:**
    Your name/logo will receive proper recognition and exposure by donating on either platform.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What perks do sponsors receive?">
    Sponsors receive awesome perks depending on their sponsorship tier:
    - Logo/name placement on the Vuetify website
    - Recognition in the README and documentation
    - Special sponsor badges on Discord
    - Priority support (higher tiers)
    - Influence on roadmap and features (enterprise tiers)
    
    Visit the sponsorship pages for detailed tier information.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I sponsor as a company?">
    Yes! We welcome both individual and corporate sponsors. Corporate sponsors receive additional benefits including:
    - Logo placement with higher visibility
    - Enterprise support options through Tidelift
    - Custom sponsorship packages available
    
    Contact us to discuss enterprise sponsorship opportunities.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Why Sponsor?

By sponsoring Vuetify, you:

- **Support Open Source:** Help maintain and improve a framework used by thousands of developers
- **Ensure Continuity:** Your support helps ensure Vuetify continues to receive updates and improvements
- **Get Recognition:** Gain visibility in the Vue.js community
- **Influence Development:** Have a say in the future direction of Vuetify
- **Give Back:** Support the open source ecosystem that powers your applications
